About Us

On-high Janitors is a Lagos based sole-proprietorship
cleaning services company registered with Corporate
Affairs Commission of Nigeria.
As professional janitors, we do not just clean but
also care for and maintain homes, residential estates,
hotels, schools, hospitals, workshops etc.

We are a registered member of the Nigerian Association of
Medium Enterprises (NASME), Lagos. The Small and Medium Enterprises
Development Agency Of Nigeria, SMEDAN, as well as the Cleaning
Practitioners Association of Nigeria, (CPAN).

Who We Are

We are custodians of home and offices facilities, hotels and hospital facilities,warehouses and workshop facilities, residential estates, car parks etc. Marble, terrazzo, parquet floor and wall surfaces and interlocking stones/concrete, are better cleaned and maintained by OnHigh Janitors.
It is important to note that cleaning and maintenance of the facilities isn't just about improving the surface, it is about changing one's entire home and offices outlook and an accession on the family and corporate lifestyle ladder. A clean and radiant floor surface is enchanting to the eyes, as it dazzles.
